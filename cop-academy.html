Cop Academy

VR (HTC Vive) Party Game in the cop universe. Used Unity3D and SteamVR. Produced with another game programmer and 3 game designers as a school project of 4 months. I produced 4 mini games and worked on the initial structure used for the overall management of the game.

  • About the background

Background

As a student to become a new cop, you must be put to the test of the Cop Academy ! Challenge tests that all the most impressive cops around the world have passed with perfect scores ! With the other students, make 2 groups and become the best cop students !

  • About my work

My work

At the beginning of the project, I proposed and worked on the flow of the game with an architecture allowing changes on anything without impacting what should not be impacted (example : changing how the mini games are played will not impact the global flow of a party in any way). I've developed several mini games : a shooting stand, a hot-cold game with a distributor, playing as a CRS member with a stick and a shield, and lastly a game where you follow the instructions of the informatic service to repair your computer.

  • Example of a VR problem

Example of a VR problem

The game with the distributor involved a problem : the fact that I wanted a precise point. Since both the colliders of the distributor and the objects you have in hand are kinematic, I had to change one of them in a trigger. The most obvious choice was the object held in the hand since it can also move other things. So I had to make the distributor's faces' colliders as triggers. But then you don't have contact point, and I resolved it by projecting the closest point of the object to the face on the distributor and this several times (like 10 times) to get a point close enough to the real contact point. The thing is such problem occurs often with VR.
