YMCA

Local multiplayer game. Used Unity3D. Made for the Global Game Jam in January 2018 with 3 other game programmers and 2 game designers in 48 hours. The theme was "Transmission". You control one of the character in the arena and you possess 4 skills. When you touch someone with a skill, you transmit it to him. The only way to win is to have no skill at all and run to the exit before someone give you any skill.

Background

We took a diversifier : YMCA of the village people. Hence the name of the game "You Must Convey Abilities". The characters are also based on the village people. About the concept of the game, I was not in the team yet when it was decided so I just followed the flow.
